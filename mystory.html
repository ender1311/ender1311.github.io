My Story

I witnessed the proliferation of the internet growing up...

I built my first computer and my first website at 15

Video games inspired me with stories of great friendships

I discovered that making friends was not always easy in reality

In college I found a group of friends that simply loved and valued me

This changed my life

I spent the next 15 years working for churches/ non-profits

My aim was to see that incredible transformation work in others

I mentored dozens of next-generation leaders every year

Recently, I decided to transition my career and pivot back to my love of tech

In order to combine my passion for technology

and my determination to bring value to as many humans as possible

Coding is just so interesting to me!

I am constantly trying to solve problems with tech

My list of things I want to learn how to do just keeps growing

Tech brings out the unending curious nature in me!

I believe that tech can be leveraged for many positive things including: education, quality of life, and human connection.
