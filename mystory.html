My Story

I witnessed the proliferation of the internet...

I built my first computer and my first website at 15.
I learned C++ at 16.

Video games inspired me with stories and connection between characters.
Sadly, I realized I did not have those in reality.
In a world where I was isolated from peers, computers offered me a way to connect.

In college, I discovered the incredible, transformative impact of a community that simply loved and valued me.
I made many close friendships.
At my wedding, I expanded my group of six groomsmen to include thirty mighty men.

That transformation in college was so profound that I spent the next 15 years working for churches/ non-profits.
My aim was to see that incredible transformation work in others.
I mentored dozens of next-generation leaders every year.

Recently, I have decided to transition into the tech industry
This gives me the opportunity to combine my passion for technology with my determination to bring value to as many humans as possible

Coding is just so interesting to me!
I constantly run into problems that can be solved with tech.
I am always trying to think of ways to automate my life.

Tech brings out the unending curious nature in me!
I believe that tech can be leveraged for many positive things: quality of life, human connection, and time spent with family and friends.
