Title: Computational Fluid Dynamics analysis of heat flux over turbine blades

Highlights:

  • Worked with legacy fortran code: TURBO

  • TURBO: a 3D, Reynolds-Averaged Navier-Stokes (RANS), computational fluid dynamics code capable of handling unsteady flows

  • Computed heat flux and pressure gradients across blades with these governing equations of fluid motion

  • CFD predictions for heat transfer was on average 15% greater and experimental results.

  • General trends of increases and decreases in heat transfer over blade surface was captured well by CFD.
